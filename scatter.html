Visualizing Voters Turnout and Margins by County(2004 to 2026)

In my continuing understand patterns of different states, I know most of states will consistently voted to specific party, and the same few swing states usually decide the election. And I want to figure out which county will impact the election and how these county changes their seats over time?

  • Encoding

    Bubble areas are proportional to the fraction of national votes

  • Interactivity
    • Filter: Click through years to see the changesin margin and turnout. Search by state or county to view a subset of counties.
    • Tooltips: Tooltips show both the county level and state level data on mouseover.
    • A dropdown menu to switch between county and state.






    • Conclusion:

      The left-right sorting is especially apparent in Midwestern swing states that gave the election to Trump. Try searching for WI, PA, MI, IA, MN and clicking through the years to see this sorting in action.
