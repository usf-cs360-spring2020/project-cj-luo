Presidental electional analysis

In this project, I am trying to seek a voting patterns of US Presidential Election by visualizing the result of the elections between 1984 and 2016. I also want to understand how seats in Congress have shifted over the years.



Why this toptic?

As an international student, I am curious about the presidental election in United State, especially in this year. I know in the US, there are only two parties considered by most voters - the Democrats (the liberal, left-of-centre party) and the Republicans (the conservative, right-of-centre party).I want to know how the different state vote between 1984 and 2016.

Visualizations

I built a geocoded electoral map in Tableau, using red for Republican states and blue for Democratic states. I included a tooltip that shows information on the winning candidate, the margin of victory, and the number of electoral votes. I also made a list of the swing states where the margin of victory was less than 5%. I created a time-series line graph to display how the voting patterns in each state have evolved over several elections.
